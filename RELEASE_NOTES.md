## 🚀 **INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS**

### **Step 1: Enable Unknown Sources**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Security** (or **Privacy**)
2. Enable **"Install from unknown sources"** or **"Unknown sources"**
3. On newer Android versions: **Settings** > **Apps** > **Special app access** > **Install unknown apps**

### **Step 2: Download & Install**
1. Download `JP-Chat-Talk-Fun-Chat-v1.0.0.apk` from this repository
2. Tap the downloaded APK file
3. Follow the installation prompts
4. Grant necessary permissions when requested

### **Step 3: Setup**
1. Open the app
2. Register with your email and create a unique username
3. Start adding friends by their usernames
4. Enjoy chatting with advanced features!
